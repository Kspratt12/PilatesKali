About Me

Welcome to Salem Street Pilates, your Hometown Studio!

Hi! I'm Kali, and my passion is Pilates! I'm a comprehensive Nationally Certified Pilates instructor. I specialize in Mat, Reformer, Cadillac, Wunda Chair, Ladder Barrel and small equipment.

As a classical ballet dancer and dance instructor, my profound understanding of body movement and body mechanics has played a vital role in my life and my teaching.

At Salem Street Pilates, you'll experience Pilates in a warm and welcoming environment. My studio is geared toward small intimate classes with a hands-on approach. I believe pilates is for everyone, and I keep that in mind when it comes to tailoring my teaching to individual clients.

It's my goal that everyone who comes to my studio leaves with a stronger understanding of not only their health and wellness, but also of themselves!
